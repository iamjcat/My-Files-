How I made my Logo

 
  

  Step 1

  Select the Text tool on Paint.net









  



  Step 2

 Select a thick font such as Arial Black







 



  Step 3

  Type two identical letters, each with a 
  different color







  



  Step 4

Then click the 'nub' at the bottom right of the  text  and move one  letter near to the other like
this.





  



  Step 5

 Type the text and be creative
 





  

  
  Step 6
 
   Click the font and select the Adjustments and 
    Effects tab. Then choose how the font looks
    at these tabs
